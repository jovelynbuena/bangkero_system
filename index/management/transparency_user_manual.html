1. Page Overview

The Transparency Dashboard is the admin/officer page for recording and managing all financial and community transparency data. It has three main sections:

SectionPurpose
AssistanceRecord money or goods/equipment received from government, NGOs, LGUs, or private partners.
ExpensesRecord how funds were spent — purchases, payments, disbursements.
Community AchievementsUpload photos of products, milestones, and outcomes shown on the public page.

At the top of the page you will also see summary stat cards:

  • Cash Assistance — total monetary assistance received (₱)
  • In-Kind (Gamit) — estimated value of goods/equipment received (₱)
  • Partners — number of unique sources that gave assistance
  • Total Expenses — total amount recorded as expenses (₱)
2. View Reports Button
— top right of the header

What it does: Opens the transparency_reports.php page where you can see printable summaries, charts, and exportable data of all assistance, expenses, and beneficiaries.

Use this when preparing reports for meetings or audits.
3. Tab Navigation

Click any tab to switch between sections. Only one section is shown at a time.

TabIconWhat You Can Do
Assistance View, add, edit, and archive assistance records (cash or in-kind)
Expenses View, add, edit, and delete expense records. Also shows cash balance.
Community Achievements View, add, edit, and archive community achievement photos.
4. Add Assistance Button
— top-right of the Assistance tab

What it does: Opens the Record Assistance modal form where you can log a new assistance entry (cash or in-kind).

This button is only visible to users with edit permissions (Admin or Treasurer).

6. Edit & Archive Assistance

Each row in the Assistance table has two action buttons in the Actions column:

ButtonWhat It Does
Edit — Opens the assistance form pre-filled with the existing data. Make changes and click Save to update.
Archive — Shows a confirmation dialog. If confirmed, moves the record to the archive (hidden from the main list). It can be restored from the Archives page.
Archiving is not permanent deletion. The record is preserved in the archive and can be restored by an Admin.
7. Add Expense Button
— top-right of the Expenses tab

What it does: Opens the Record Expense modal where you can log a disbursement or purchase made using the association's funds.

Above the table you will also see a summary strip showing:

  • Total Expenses — sum of all expenses recorded
  • Cash Received — total cash assistance received
  • Cash Balance — Cash Received minus Total Expenses (shown in red if negative)

9. Edit & Delete Expense

Each row in the Expenses table has two action buttons:

ButtonWhat It Does
Edit — Opens the expense form pre-filled. Make changes and click Save Expense to update.
Delete — Shows a confirmation dialog. If confirmed, the expense is permanently deleted. This cannot be undone.
Unlike assistance records, deleting an expense is permanent. Double-check before confirming.

11. Edit & Archive Achievement

Each achievement card has two action buttons at the bottom:

ButtonWhat It Does
Edit — Opens the achievement form. You can update the title, caption, tag, sort order, and add more images. Existing images are shown with a delete (×) button to remove individual photos.
Archive — Shows a confirmation dialog. If confirmed, the achievement is moved to the archive and hidden from the public page.
When editing, you can remove individual images using the × button on each thumbnail. New images you upload are appended — existing images are kept unless deleted.

13. Tips & Reminders
Always fill in required fields
Fields marked with * (Source Name and Amount / Items) must be filled before saving. The form will not submit if these are empty.
You can upload multiple images
On both Assistance and Achievement forms, hold Ctrl (Windows) or Cmd (Mac) while selecting files in the file picker to choose multiple images at once.
Link expenses to assistance sources
When adding an expense, use the Linked to Assistance Source dropdown to connect it to the fund it came from. This makes the financial trail clear for audits.
Archive ≠ Delete
Archiving an assistance record or achievement moves it to a hidden archive — it is not permanently deleted. Only expenses can be permanently deleted.
In-Kind total auto-calculates
When recording Gamit/In-Kind assistance, the system multiplies Qty × Unit Value for each item and shows the estimated total at the bottom of the items table. You do not need to compute manually.
Cash Balance warning
If Total Expenses exceeds Cash Received, the Cash Balance will show in red. This means more has been spent than received in cash — review your expense and assistance records.
